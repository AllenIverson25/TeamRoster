Team History

The 2001-2002 Philadelphia 76ers were coming off an NBA Finals appearance in the previous season where they faced the Los Angeles Lakers.

Led by Allen Iverson, the reigning MVP, the team was known for their gritty defensive style and "never say die" attitude. The addition of defensive powerhouse Dikembe Mutombo midway through the previous season reinforced their identity as a defensive-minded team.

Despite high expectations following their Finals run, the 2001-2002 season was challenging due to injuries, with Allen Iverson missing 22 games. The team finished with a 43-39 record and lost in the first round of the playoffs to the Boston Celtics.
