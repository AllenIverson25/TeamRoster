Team Highlights

The 2001-2002 Philadelphia 76ers featured one of the most exciting players in NBA history - Allen Iverson.

Despite standing at just 6'0", Iverson was a scoring machine, leading the league with 31.4 points per game during the 2001-2002 season. His crossover dribble, quickness, and fearless drives to the basket made him nearly impossible to guard.

The team was built around Iverson's talents, with defensive specialists like Dikembe Mutombo, Eric Snow, and Aaron McKie complementing his offensive prowess.
